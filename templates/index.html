App for controlling industrial machineries

The functionality of this site consists in controlling a servo motor, a step motor through just a click, also deciding the angle of rotation. There is the possibility to get parameters through a DHT11 sensor.

Servo Motor

Servo Motor machine offers the possibility to rotate the previously inserted propeller,
by 90 and/or 180 degrees.




Step Motor

Step Motor machine offers the possibility to decide the rotation time, the angle
and the clockwise or counterclockwise direction.

DHT11 Sensor

The DHT11 Sensor offers the possibility to get the temperature and humidity parameters of the
surrounding environment.
